Every year in the City of Orinda, residents with Alzheimer's, dementia, or Down Syndrom walk away from their homes or caring facilities.

  "At Risk" is a online profile system to help our police officers in organize searches when somebody goes missing.

  If you are a family member, friend, or caregiver to such a person, please give "At Risk" a try to help our police officers organize searches.
